SIT Housing

Are you looking for a place to live? SIT provides affordable and student-oriented housing options in Trondheim. From apartments to shared flats, their well-equipped accommodations offer convenience and comfort. With multiple locations in the city, SIT ensures easy access to campus and city life for students. Get an overview of your possibilities here and find the perfect housing for you!

Berg studentby

Berg student village is perfect for students that study at NTNU Gløshaugen and want a social and fun place to live. Berg student village is the nearest neighbor to NTNU Gløshaugen and within walking distance of NTNU Campus Elgeseter. There is also excellent bus connectivity to the city center and NTNU Dragvoll

Moholt studentby

Moholt student village is Trondheim's largest student community! it is renowned for its vibrant and diverse international atmosphere. The student village is located within walking and biking distance from both NTNU's Dragvoll and Gløshaugen and is in close proximity to NTNUs Moholt campus

Lerkendal studentby

Lerkendal student village is conveniently located within walking distance of both Gløshaugen and Øya campuses, and it offers excellent public transportation connections to all other campuses across the city.

Teknobyen studentby

Teknobyen student village consist of 116 students that lives in both single rooms or double apartments, each with private bathrooms. Together they share a big kitchen, a common living area, and a veranda. This is the perfect place for those who are social and outgoing, looking to connect with a diverse group of individuals.
