SiT Trening

SiT trening offers the most affordable gym-membership for students. With 4 different locations, you can live nearly everywhere and still live close to one of the gyms. Depending on which gym you visit, there are different activities to do such as running, spinning, weight lifting, yoga and much more!

SiT Gløshaugen

Sit Gløshaugen is SiT’s biggest gym. It’s perfect for an intense workout as it is permitted to drop weights to the floor. This gym also offers a varerity of classes, such as sprints, step-classes and yoga. SiT Gløshaugen also has bigger halls for playing sports such as futsal.

SiT Portalen

SiT Portalen is located near Solsiden and has both treadmills, cycles and weight training-equipment. This gym also offers classes such as cycle-classes and yoga.

SiT Dragvoll

SiT Dragvoll is located at Dragvoll. It has both weight training-equipement, cycles, treadmills and a big hall for playing sports. This gym also has some classes including spinning and yoga.

SiT Moholt

SiT Moholt is the smallest gym of them all. There is no staff working here, so you need to bring your student ID-card in order to enter the gym. This is a perfect gym if you want a quick workout in. The gym houses both treadmills, a few cycles and weight-training equipment. Unlike the other, this gym does not offer any classes.
