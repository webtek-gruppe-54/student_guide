SIT Housing

Are you looking for a place to live? SIT provides affordable and student-oriented housing options in Trondheim. From apartments to shared flats, their well-equipped accommodations offer convenience and comfort. With multiple locations in the city, SIT ensures easy access to campus and city life for students. Get an overview of your possibilities here and find the perfect housing for you!

Dromedar Kaffebar

Dromedar is a renowned coffee chain in Trondheim, celebrated for its commitment to high-quality coffee. The atmosphere is relaxed, making it an ideal spot for both work and leisure.

Baklandet Skydsstation

Located in a historic building, this café exudes Old World charm. Enjoy freshly baked bread, pastries, and hearty Norwegian dishes in a cozy, rustic atmosphere.

Cafe-Sito Realfagsbygget

SiT’s own coffee-bar, offering coffees, tea, mineral drinks and a wide variety of pastries and fruit for a quick snack on the go. Offered at student-friendly prices and located in the heart of Realfagsbygget.
