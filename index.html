    • Purpose and goal of website

    This website has two main goals. First, it's here to help NTNU freshmen get to know Trondheim better. We want them to be familiar with all the different places and attractions in the city. Second, we want to provide information about the student housing options offered by SIT. This way, students can make informed decisions about where to live while studying at NTNU. Ultimately, we aim to make the transition to university life in Trondheim as smooth as possible for our new students.

    Audience

    Our primary audience for this website is the incoming freshmen at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim. These students are embarking on an exciting new chapter in their lives, often away from their hometowns and familiar surroundings. These freshmen may come from various backgrounds and have diverse interests, so our goal is to provide them with a comprehensive guide that caters to their needs. We aim to make them more knowledgeable about the different facets of Trondheim, including shopping, recreational activities, socializing opportunities, and housing options provided by SIT (Studentersamfundet i Trondhjem). By addressing their unique needs and curiosities, we aim to ensure a smooth and enjoyable transition into university life in Trondheim for these new students.

    Specific Preferences

  • Simplicity and Clean Design: SiT is looking for a website with a clean and simple design that is easy for users to navigate. The website should offer a user-friendly experience, ensuring that visitors can quickly find the information they need.
  • Visual Appeal: SiT aims for a visually appealing website. This means incorporating aesthetically pleasing design elements, color schemes, and graphics to create an attractive site.
  • Illustrative Imagery: SiT wants to use pictures to showcase the various activities and attractions available in Trondheim. These images will serve as visual aids to help users better understand what they can experience in the city, making the website more engaging and informative.

    We want to put lots of pictures on our website, but we know too many can make the site slow and annoying for users. So, we'll make sure to use smaller and fewer pictures to keep everything running smoothly and hassle-free.
